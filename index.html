How the scoring works

  • Sun score now: based on terrace orientation vs. current selected sun azimuth.
  • Evening / afternoon score: precomputed checks at 18:00 and 15:00.
  • Distance score: shorter distance boosts ranking when user location is available.
  • Confidence: terrace existence + orientation certainty from source tags and heuristics.
  • Terrace type bonus: rooftop and waterfront get a small visibility bonus.
